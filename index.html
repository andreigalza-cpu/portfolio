Adobo

Adobo is a famous Filipino dish made with chicken or pork cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. It has a savory and slightly sour taste and is usually served with rice.

Sinigang

A sour Filipino soup usually cooked with pork, vegetables, and tamarind. It has a refreshing sour taste and is often served hot with rice.

Ginataang Saging

A sweet Filipino dish made with bananas cooked in coconut milk and sugar. It is creamy, sweet, and often eaten as a snack or dessert.
